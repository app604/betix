## Description
Betix Sports & Trainings turns your workouts into clear, motivating data. Built for athletes who want signal, not noise, Betix combines a focused performance dashboard, a structured weekly plan, progress analytics, and a light "bet on yourself" streak system that keeps you accountable session after session. No gambling, no clutter, no guesswork.

Open the app to a single performance index that summarizes your last seven days of training load and consistency at a glance. Plan your week, log every session in seconds, and watch your strength, endurance, and stability trends take shape across clean, readable charts.

WHY BETIX

- One number that matters. A 0 to 100 performance index blends your weekly training load with how consistently you show up, so you always know whether to push or recover.
- Plan day by day or by the week. Build a schedule, assign a focus and target duration to each session, and check it off as you go.
- See real progress. Weekly load charts for strength, endurance, and stability reveal where you are improving and where you are coasting.
- Keep the streak alive. Every logged session extends your run. Track your current and best streaks and bet on yourself to keep going.
- Goals with live rings. Set targets in any unit, sessions, kilometers, kilograms, hours, and watch circular progress fill as you close in.
- A minimal session timer. A clean countdown with quick presets and round tracking for intervals, rest, and focused work blocks.

DESIGNED FOR FOCUS

Betix uses a premium dark interface with soft gradients, glass surfaces, refined typography, and a clear data hierarchy. Everything is tuned for fast logging and effortless reading, on the gym floor or on the move.

WORKS THE WAY YOU TRAIN

All of your data lives on your device and persists between launches. Adjust in-app brightness, manage notifications, and read the privacy policy from a clean, system-style settings screen.

Start with an empty slate, log your first session, and let Betix turn your effort into momentum.
